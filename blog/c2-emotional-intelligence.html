How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I have been trying out the Headspace App over the last few days and found the two minutes of mindfulness very similar uses very similar techniques to that found in the app. Both initially focus getting you to focus your mind on your body (such as your breathing) and then allowing it to wonder and observe your thoughts and the sounds, smells and sensations around you. It is an interesting experience although while it sounds easy, in practice I found it a bit tricky to keep focused, however and generally I felt a little calmer after performing these techniques.
