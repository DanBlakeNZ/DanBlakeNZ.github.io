Search Inside Yourself - Chapter 5

27 January 2017


When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

I think when programming then fustration can be triggered if a section of code is not working as expected and it becomes difficult to find/see the cause. This can cause fustration as it can become difficult to work on a solution. I think in programming it is often the little things that are easily overlooked that can cause the biggest headaches, especially when you are learning. When you are learning you are often trying to figure out many things at once so sometimes its not always obvious what is wrong, is your syntax and logic all wrong, or are you simply missing something small like a bracket - all of this can cause the feeling of fustration.


How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

This exercise helped to calm the mind, and let go of any negitive feelings inside the body. Being able to take some time out to notice and acknoledge the feeling, allows you to then be able tackle the feeling and the problem that caused it to well up in the first place. This should enable you to have a clear(er) mind, which will allow you to focus on your actions and response.
