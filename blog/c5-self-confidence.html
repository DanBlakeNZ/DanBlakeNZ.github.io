Search Inside Yourself - Chapter 3

19 January 2017


What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfullness and Self Awareness are very much the same thing, it is being aware of your actions and emotions and view them objectivly - like we are viewing ourselves in the thrid person. This can help you respond better in certain situations as you are aware of you limits, which in turn can help increase self confidence.


Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried the body scan method, and honnestly didn't feel much... just that my mind kept wondering! I guess its one of those things that comes with practice.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

That you are not made up of your emotioins. Your emotions are simply what we experience in the body in reaction to something. Emotioins are physiologial experiences that can, with practice, be controlled and acted on as they are not part of your core.
